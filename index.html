How to play Trigram

  • A Trigram is a sequence of 3 letters, like CAT
  • Make words containing the Trigram of the week.
  • First a 4-letter word, then a 5-letter word, then 6... and so on. See how far you can go!

Example

Trigram of the day: CAT

  • 4-letter word: CATS
  • 5-letter word: CATER
  • 6-letter word: CATSUP
  • 7-letter word: SCATTER
  • 8-letter word: DELICATE
  • 9-letter word: DELICATES
  • Can you continue the streak??

Notes

  • Plurals and other suffixes (like -ed and -ing) are perfectly valid!
  • Words must contain the Trigram (like "CAT") as is, with no letters in between.
